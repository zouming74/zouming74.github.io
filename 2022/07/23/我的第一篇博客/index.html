数学公式

The Stone-Weierstrass Theorem: If $ f $ is a continuous complex function on [a,b],there exists a sequence of polynomials \(P_n\) such that \[ \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}P_n(x)=f(x) \] uniformly on [a,b].If $ f $ is real,the \(P_n\) may be taken real.
